FrogCult SMP Rules!

Breaking any of the following rules may result in a server or community ban, so please follow them.

  • [1] Usernames and skins must be appropriate.
  • [2] Griefing is strictly prohibited and will result in a permanent ban.
  • [3] Do not hack or cheat it will result in a permanent ban, THIS INCLUDES XRAY datapacks.
  • [4] If you are having issues with another player or are uncomfortable with their messages, please notify a mod or report it here.
  • [5] Any type of harassment is not allowed.
  • [6] Never share your account information with anyone.
  • [7] This is a shared community, so please keep the chat friendly for all ages.
  • [8]Profanity towards another, excessive swearing, racism, and discrimination are prohibited.
  • [9] Stealing another players items is not allowed and is considered griefing.
  • [10] Ask before you build next to someone.
  • [11] Be polite and show respect to each other.
  • [12] Have fun!

How To...

On the FrogCult SMP you can have up to 2 homes.

Set your first home by doing:

/sethome [HomeName1]

Or simply just saying

/sethome

Set your second home by doing:

/sethome [HomeName2]

Teleport to your first home with (This only works if you have one home set):

/home

Otherwise to get to the respective homes do:

/home 1
/home 2

Or

/home [HomeName]

On the FrogCult SMP you can have multiple claims.

To claim land you first need to craft a golden shovel or optain them from the spawn tent:

/warp spawn

To get to the spawn island then walk into the tent below.


Once you have a golden shovel simply hold it and right click one corner of the land you want to claim

A diamond block will appear marking the first corner of the claim.

Move to the otherside of the land you want to claim and right click again with the shovel in hand.

A rectangle (like below) should appear and this is the outline of your claim!



To trust other players:

/trust [player_name]

To view all trusted players do:

/trustlist

To abandon claim, stand in the claim you wish to abandon and do:

/abandonclaim

To abandon all claims do:

/abandonallclaims

For more information check out the Claim plugins wiki here:

https://github.com/bloodmc/GriefDefender/wiki

On the FrogCult SMP there are multiple warps you can use.

To get to the server spawn do:

/warp spawn

To get to the server town/shop district do:

/warp town

To get below the server spawn, aka the ground do:

/warp ground

There are also some other places you can go to from the town like the end portal or even a gold farm.

Go to the town and you will see a portal room:

/warp town

To teleport to these places try jumping through the paintings and explore:

And remember you can always get back home or to town with:

/warp town
/home
